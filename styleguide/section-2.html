Colors

Colors Pallete use for the design and develoment process. The colors variables are in the HTML comments tags.

Example:

$primary-light-color: #FAF5E7;

2.1 Primary

<div class="color-swatches">
  <div class="color-swatch primary-light-color"></div> <!-- $primary-light-color: #FAF5E7; -->
  <div class="color-swatch primary-dark-color"></div>  <!-- $primary-dark-color: #412819; -->
  <div class="color-swatch primary-black-color"></div> <!-- $primary-black-color: #1E1E1E; -->
</div>

2.2 Secondary

<div class="color-swatches">
  <div class="color-swatch primary-accent-color"></div>  <!-- $primary-accent-color: #99640B; -->
  <div class="color-swatch secondary-accent-color"></div>  <!-- $secondary-accent-color: #C34700; -->
</div>

2.3 Tertiary

<div class="color-swatches">
  <div class="color-swatch tertiary-color"></div>  <!-- $tertiary-color: #BD9F65; -->
</div>
